Projects

Lords of Noise

Based in Nashville, TN. Lords of Noise is a Hip Hop/Electronic duo in collaboration with rapper Lord Luud. Founded in late 2016, we spent months in the studio creating the 5-track EP "The Deep End" which released in February of 2017.
In the months following the release, we released a music video for the track "Wavin" from our EP, in which I took on the task of directing, shooting, and editing.

Default Noise

"Default Noise" was the pseudonym I adopted for the release of my early experimental electronic music, circa 2013.
Nearly half a decade later, I've continued pushing myself as an artist and grow my sound, while continuing to release songs and experimental tracks under the name.
